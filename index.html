Welcome to Silver Molten

Wonderful hotel in the heart of Ambernath.



The Silver Molten being inaugurated on 24th August 2018, is a wonderful setting of refreshment an oasis of isolation at the end of a fulfilling day of business or sightseeing, situated at the final point of Ambernath Lord of Sky, the S3 Park serves the ambience with abundance of natural light that flows through the tremendous skylight and large floor-to-ceiling windows along with tiled flawless red granites. Silver Molten is a progression of rare and beautiful ingredients where texture, flavour and harmony is paramount.Continually inspired by nature, the creative process for chefs begins in working closely with the farmers, fishermen, producers and artisans who cultivate bespoke produce exclusively for Silver Molten. For each dish, the growth of every element and the selection of every ceramic piece is carefully considered and crafted for its role in the dining experience.
